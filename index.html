What Are These Modeling?
The Model: Hypersphere growth from origin using (2r)³·2π·e, yielding the fine structure constant α⁻¹ ≈ 137 at r=1. Paired antipodal poles represent fundamental two-body constraints.

What's Demonstrated: Concentration of measure in high dimensions, polynomial growth in shell volumes, two contrasting distribution methods on the hypersphere surface.

Distribution Methods: Spiral shows coherent/ordered evolution (phonons, crystals, quantum coherence). Stochastic shows decoherent/disordered evolution (thermal states, diffusion, entropy).
